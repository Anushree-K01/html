Delicious Almond Sweet recipe

Welcome to this easy and delicious almond sweet recipe.

This healthy, sweet treat combines almonds with a rich flavor. It's simple to make and perfect for any occasion. Let's begin!

Ingredients:

  • 1 cup almonds
  • 1/4 cup sugar
  • 1/4 cup water
  • 1/2 tsp cardamom powder
  • A pinch of saffron
  • 1 tsp ghee
  • Chopped almonds for garnish

Instructions:

  1. Boil almonds for 2 minutes, then peel the skins.
  2. Grind the almonds into a thick paste.
  3. Heat sugar and water in the pan, simmer for five minutes to make a syrup.
  4. Pour the syrup into the paste, stir until it thickens.
  5. Add cardamom powder and saffron, mix well.
  6. Once thick, transfer to a plate and let it cool.
  7. Shape into squares and garnish with chopped almonds.
  8. Let it set

Enjoy your homemade almond sweet!
